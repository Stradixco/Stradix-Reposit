About Us

Stradix specializes in marine services, maintenance, general contracting, and supplying quality materials and spare parts. We also offer specialized technicians, infrastructure solutions, digital products, and business establishment support to meet our clients’ needs efficiently.

Comprehensive Marine Services

At Stradix, we offer a wide range of marine services, including maintenance and repair of marine equipment and vessels, ensuring the efficiency and reliability of our clients’ marine operations. Our team of specialized technicians brings extensive experience and in-depth knowledge of the latest industry standards and technologies, allowing us to provide tailored solutions to meet each client’s unique needs. We are committed to delivering the highest quality in our services, ensuring safety, sustainability, and operational efficiency in all marine projects we manage.

General Contracting and Infrastructure

Stradix is a trusted partner in general contracting, providing comprehensive solutions in the design and execution of major infrastructure projects. Expertise to manage all aspects of projects, including planning, design, and execution, while ensuring adherence to timelines and budgets. We also provide business establishment support, helping clients launch their projects with confidence and stability, backed by our in-depth market knowledge and ability to deliver solutions that meet their specific needs.
