Our Services

we offer comprehensive marine services, including maintenance, general contracting, and the supply of materials and spare parts. Our services also extend to providing specialized technicians, full infrastructure solutions, digital products, and business establishment support to ensure our clients’ success.

01

Comprehensive Marine Maintenance and Supply Solutions

Stradix provides specialized marine maintenance services with a skilled team in welding, piping, painting, and mechanical repairs. We supply essential spare parts and handle used oil collection, marine sludge disposal, and mixed water waste management.

Additionally, we supply fresh food material provisions for vessels, ensuring efficient and reliable support for all marine operational needs. Our commitment to quality and environmental responsibility ensures that each service we provide meets the highest standards in the industry.

02

Material supply for Marine and Industrial Needs

Stradix offers a wide range of supply services to meet the diverse needs of marine and industrial operations. Our services include the supply of general construction materials, rental of tools and equipment, and certified technical inspections.

We also provide a full range of marine spare parts, fresh drinking water, and high-quality fresh food provisions, ensuring that our clients have access to essential resources for smooth and efficient operations.
